STYLING SESSION: WOLFORD

THE LITTLE BLACK DRESS

May I introduce you to this little black dress (if you are not yet familiar, it’s a popular one from Wolford). At first I was concerned whether I would fit in considering how bloated I feel lately, even though I am working out everyday and man I swear I am giving my best for looking good… for myself, obviously! But still, you know us girls, there are days that it just doesn’t feel right. Thankfully, not much effort is needed as the dress comes in a “tube” form and you can style it up x ways and however you want it to look… so you can pull it up, a bit here, and a bit there and voilá, makes the perfect dress for you!
