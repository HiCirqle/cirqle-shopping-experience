Grey Basic Shirt

posted 06-27-2015 category TRENDS

The grey basic shirt has solidified its place amongst bloggers as a chic street style piece that is anything but basic. When paired with black separates or sultry heels, this sophisticated staple becomes the centerpiece of an updated monochromatic look; a welcomed change from total white or all black ubiquitous outfits. Plus, a reliable grey short-sleeve shirt is perfect for pulling off an effortless “I Woke Up Like This” get-up, where cozy is fueling the minimalistic trend. Unlike most super trendy items that quickly sell out and become obsolete, a grey top is easily replaceable and can be kept in rotation from season to season.
