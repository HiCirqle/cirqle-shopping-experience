My Sunglasses Collection


Firstly, I do not have an addiction.
That is what I keep being told whenever I bring home a new pair of sunglasses. What can I say? I have a thing for them. That's all. It is not an addiction.

For years now I have been obsessed with finding sunglasses that are a little unusual or that I just love. So I thought that I would share some (yep, there is more) of my ultimate favourites with you. Some of them are from last Summer, so I do apologise if you fall in love with them and I wish you all the luck trying to hunt them down now. 




Warehouse 2014

TKMaxx 2014

New Look 2014

New Look 2015

New Look 2015

Majority of these sunglasses are from New Look. I love how far the New Look range has developed and their costs haven't increased massively. The average price for these sunglasses are around £5.99 a pair - that is nothing compared to some High Street stores.

The last pair in this collection is the newest - and I think that they are my ultimate favourite or at least they are, until another pair come along.  I am a sucker for rounded lens with thick frames or a cat eye lens, but there must be another small detail that draws me in. For the last pair it is the blue tones lens, the clear frame with the spots and the \"invisible\" bridge - even though it is clearly visible haha. I also love how all of these sunglasses have a modern twist on a vintage style. That is my go to style.

What is your \"go to \" sunglasses style? Vintage/Pilot/Round/Cateye?
